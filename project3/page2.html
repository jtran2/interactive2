DAY BEFORE

Mua hai bình hoa cúc.

The preparation for Lunar New Year is as—if not more—exhausting as the day itself. Lots of money goes into the planning of the event. Some of the most distinct parts of Vietnamese New Year are the decorations and ornaments, as well as the abundance of fruits and flowers.

Bỏ trái bưởi lên bàn thờ.

One of the most important rituals on this day is being the first person to enter a home, which is called xông nhà. Traditions say that the first visitor a family receives in the New Year determines their fortune for the rest of the year. On this day, people never enter other houses until being invited inside.
